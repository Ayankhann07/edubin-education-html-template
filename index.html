About us

Welcome to

All Saints'

All Saints' College of Science & Commerce has been a center of educational excellence for over 47 years. Located in the heart of the city, the college is committed to nurturing academic brilliance while promoting Indian cultural values, social responsibility, and national goals. With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and innovative teaching methods, the college provides an environment where students can grow academically and personally.

The institution offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs including B.Com, BBA, BCA, and M.Com with specialized training in finance, management, and technology. Emphasis is placed on personality development through seminars, workshops, spoken English classes, and career-focused training like competitive exam preparation and accounting software. Scholarships and support services ensure inclusive education for all.
