Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)

Offered by All Saints College of Science & Commerce, Bhopal

Overview

The BBA program at All Saints College focuses on building strong managerial, communication, and leadership skills. It provides in-depth knowledge in areas like management, marketing, finance, and human resources to prepare students for corporate careers or entrepreneurial ventures.

Duration:

3 Years (6 Semesters)

Eligibility:

10+2 (Any Stream) from a recognized board

Affiliation:

Barkatullah University, Bhopal

Subjects Offered:

  • ✔ Principles of Management
  • ✔ Business Communication
  • ✔ Financial Accounting
  • ✔ Marketing Management
  • ✔ Organizational Behavior
  • ✔ Human Resource Management
  • ✔ Business Law

Career Opportunities:
