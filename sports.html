Sports & Physical Activities

Promoting Fitness, Teamwork & Sportsmanship

Overview

At All Saints College of Science & Commerce, we believe in the holistic development of students. Our sports program encourages active participation in both indoor and outdoor games, helping students stay fit, focused, and foster team spirit. Regular sports events, inter-college tournaments, and physical training sessions are a part of campus life.

Facilities Include:
  • ✔ Cricket and Football Grounds
  • ✔ Basketball and Volleyball Courts
  • ✔ Table Tennis & Badminton Hall
  • ✔ Gym & Fitness Equipment
  • ✔ Annual Sports Day & Inter-College Competitions
